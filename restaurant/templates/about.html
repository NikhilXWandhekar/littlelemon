About Us

Based in Tathawade, Pune, Little Lemon is a family owned veg restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a Mahrashtrian twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Punjab,Udupi, and Kerala culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Lemon is owned by two DESI brothers, Ramesh and Suresh, who moved to the Maharashtra to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Suresh relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Delhi.

Ramesh does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic INDIAN to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mahrashtrian region.
